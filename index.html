    About Us

    Established with a passion for holistic education, Abhyasa is dedicated to nurturing young minds, fostering a love for learning, and instilling values that guide our students towards success in both academic and real-world pursuits.

    • Founded in 2004, Abhyasa evolved from a vision of education.
    • Proud legacy, prioritizing innovation, fostering excellence in every student.
    • Rooted in Vinayak Nagar, Nizamabad, shaping futures since its inception.

    Infrastructure and Facilities

    Abhyasa The School boasts modern and spacious classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a comprehensive library, and state-of-the-art computer facilities. Our campus also features dedicated areas for sports, recreation, and extracurricular activities, providing students with a stimulating and conducive environment for holistic development

    • 01 How can I apply for admission?

      You can download the admission form from our website or visit the school office to collect a hard copy. Complete the form and submit it along with the required documents during the admission period.

    • 02 What is the curriculum followed at Abhyasa The School? nunc?

      We follow a comprehensive curriculum designed to meet academic standards, foster critical thinking, and encourage practical application of knowledge. Our curriculum is regularly updated to align with educational advancements.

    • 03 What are the school hours?

      School hours are from 9:15am to 4:00pm, Monday to Saturday. Any changes or additional activities will be communicated in advance through circulars or the school's official communication channels.
